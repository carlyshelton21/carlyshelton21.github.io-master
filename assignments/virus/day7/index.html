MARCH 27
IT WAS FINALLY OKAY FOR ME TO DRIVE AROUND SINCE DAD FILLED UP MY CAR.
I WENT FOR A LONG DRIVE INTO VIRGINIA ALONE WITH MY WINDOWS DOWN BLASTING NOISE. I GOT AN OLD SENSE OF FREEDOM BUT ALSO CONSTRAINT.
A RESULT OF NOT BEING ABLE TO STOP AND EXPLORE. YET, IT WAS DEFINITELY ENJOYABLE TO SEE MORE OF THE WORLD THAN JUST THE SURROUNDINGS OF MY CHILDHOOD HOME FOR A COUPLE FREEING HOURS.
