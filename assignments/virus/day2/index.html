MARCH 22
YESTERDAY, WHEN I ARRIVED IN NORTH CAROLINA THE AIRPORT WAS FULL. I FELT SUFFOCATED BY THE LACK OF SERIOUSNESS. MY PARENTS PICKED ME UP AND BEGAN
TO SMOTHER ME WITH QUESTIONS. ON THE WAY HOME WE PICKED UP GROCERIES AT
THE LOCAL GROCERY STORE. THERE WERE MANY PEOPLE THERE TOO. YET THIS TIME THEY ALL KNEW ME AND WERE DYING TO
INTEGRATE ME. I FELT IMPRISONED BY THEIR CONVERSATIONS FROM MY RECENT LACK
OF INTERACTION. I POLITELY JUST ENDED UP WAVING AT THEM WITH MY SWEATY LATEX GLOVED HANDS.
